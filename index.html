About

We offer various programs, from a mini bootcamp to give you a taste to see if front-end development is right for you, to a Job Guarantee Bootcamp where you will learn all the necessary skills, create a portfolio and a professional Linked-In presence, receive intern experience, have interview practice, and get a job making at least $60,000/year in under 12 weeks!

We have coaches, meetings, and a full community of support. Our supplemental curriculum changes to reflect the latest in-demand skills.

Student Community

We have a Community Board right within our classroom platform. Build relationships with staff and other students. Check out each other's projects. Give and get help and encouragement. Ask questions.

Personal Msg Board

You will also have access to a personal Community Board on the Slack platform. There you will have access to your Coaches, the Technical Support Team, and the Administrative personnel who signed you up and did the on-boarding with you. Coaching is an important part of the bootcamp, and here you will be able to stay in touch and be able to keep track of your conversations.
